About The Club

We are dedicated to positively promoting all aspects of motorcycling with an emphasis on Safety and Rider awareness. At HYDERABAD BC we do not care what you choose to ride, only that you love to ride! Hyderabad bikers Club is open to all brands/types of motorcycles (150 CC and above). The club welcomes both beginners as well as experienced riders, we have a corner marking rule so no one gets lost. Information on all rides and other upcoming club activities are posted to members and updated in the site. We ride together as much as we can, join others to support their events and organize fund raising efforts to help out those in our community who are in need.

HYDERABAD-KARNATAKA

The Biligiriranga Hills, commonly called B R Hills, is a hill range situated in south-eastern Karnataka, in South India. The area is called Biligiriranga Swamy Temple Wildlife Sanctuary. It is a protected reserve under the Wildlife Protection Act of 1972. Being at the confluence of the Western Ghats and the Eastern Ghats, the sanctuary is home to eco-systems that are unique to both the mountain ranges. The site was declared a Tiger Reserve in JUNE 2015.

HYDERABAD-HIMALAYAN

This epic journey is the very essence of the “High Himalayas” and we are going at it for the second time – this time with a difference. We take some of the less explored trails of this beautiful land to make this epic journey unique and memorable. Traversing some of the most spectacular landscapes on the planet, including one of the most dangerous roads in the world, every kilometre is breathtaking. Starting our journey in the pristine valleys and beautiful lakes of Kashmir we go on to some offbeat and lesser taken routes through the mesmerising dry mountain topography of Ladakh with its high altitude lakes kissing the borders of Pakistan and China and finally ending in the lower Himalayas in Himachal. Riding on this 17 day adventure it is going to be a one of a kind experience. And mind it, this is not your regular Ladakh Ride. This is THR, Part 2!

RIDER Rules

  • Helmet is a must for RIDER and PILLION.
  • Shoes and protective gears like jackets etc to protect you from any fall.
  • Always ride in a group as we appreciate riders to ride in a group unless there is any emergency or nature's call.
  • Strictly Follow Group Riding, It means we follow certain level of riding skills and self discipline. It also restricts the riders from number of stoppages and be within speed limits.
  • We'll be riding at 70kmph (avg).
  • Use HAND SIGNALLING to guide the rider behind you and also relay the signals which is given to you by the rider ahead of you.
  • KEEP AN EYE ON YOUR MIRROR to check the rider behind you.
  • Maintain Tyre Pressure.
  • NO DRINK AND DRIVE.
  • Carry Cash to meet any unforeseen eventualities.
